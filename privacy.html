1. What information we collect

The information we collect depends on how you use Mirr. Here's a complete breakdown:

If you're a customer browsing or shopping on Mirr

  • Account information — name and email address, if you create an account
  • Body measurements — any measurements you enter (chest, waist, hips, length, inseam, in cm or inches) to receive fit scores
  • Photos you upload — any photo you upload for the virtual try-on feature
  • Order information — items purchased, delivery address, order history
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe; Mirr never sees or stores your full card details
  • Device and usage information — browser type, device type, pages visited, and general usage patterns, collected automatically

If you're a brand listing products on Mirr

  • Business contact details — name, email, business name
  • Product information — product names, descriptions, prices, photos, garment dimensions, available sizes
  • Payout information — bank or payout details, collected and held by Stripe Connect, not by Mirr directly

3. Your photos and virtual try-on

THE SHORT VERSION

When you upload a photo to try on a garment, that photo is sent to PixelAPI, a third-party virtual try-on technology provider, to generate your result. We don't use your photo for anything beyond generating that try-on image and (if you choose to save your result) showing it back to you.

In more detail: when you upload a photo for virtual try-on, it is sent securely to PixelAPI (pixelapi.com), the technology provider Mirr uses to generate try-on images. PixelAPI processes the photo to merge it with the selected garment image and returns the result to Mirr, which displays it to you.

  • We do not use your photo for facial recognition, identity verification, or any purpose beyond generating the try-on result you requested
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your photo with brands, advertisers, or any party other than PixelAPI for the specific purpose of generating your try-on result
  • Photos uploaded for try-on are not permanently stored on Mirr's servers beyond what's needed to display your result during your session, unless you explicitly choose to save a result to your account
  • You can request deletion of any saved try-on photos at any time by emailing hello@trymirr.com

5. How long we keep your information

  • Try-on photos — not retained beyond your active session unless you choose to save a result; saved results are kept until you delete them or close your account
  • Account information — retained for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward in case you wish to reactivate it
  • Order history — retained for as long as required for tax, accounting, and dispute-resolution purposes (typically 5 years under Australian tax record-keeping requirements)
  • Brand product and business data — retained for as long as the brand is listed on Mirr, and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping

6. How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. This includes using secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) across the Platform, relying on reputable, security-vetted third-party providers (Stripe, PixelAPI) rather than building our own payment or AI infrastructure, and limiting access to personal information to what's needed to operate Mirr.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

7. Your rights and choices

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate or out of date
  • Request deletion of your personal information, including saved try-on photos, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records (such as order history for tax purposes)
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time
  • Ask questions about how your information is being used

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@trymirr.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, generally within 30 days.

8. Information for brand partners

Mirr does not use your product photography, pricing, or business information for any purpose outside operating the Platform, and does not share it with competing brands or third parties, except as described in Section 4.

9. Cookies and tracking

10. Children's privacy

Mirr is not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@trymirr.com and we will take steps to delete it.

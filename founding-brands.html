COMMON QUESTIONS
What does 8% commission actually mean for my business?
For every sale that comes through Mirr, we take 8¢ in the dollar — you keep 92¢. There are no monthly platform fees, no setup costs, and no charges for virtual try-ons. Mirr only earns when you do.
How does the virtual try-on work for my products?
You upload photos of your garments. When a shopper visits your listing, they upload a photo of themselves and our AI composites the garment onto their body in about a minute. The shopper sees themselves wearing your piece before they buy. We handle all the AI infrastructure.
What do you need from me to get started?
Product photos (front-facing, plain background), pricing, and a short brand description. We’ll walk you through the product upload process after you submit — it usually takes less than an hour to get your first products live.
When and how do I get paid?
Payouts go directly to your bank account via Stripe. We’re finalising the payment schedule now — founding brands will be the first to know, and will have direct input on what works for them.
Can I leave if it’s not working?
Yes. There’s no lock-in contract and no exit fee. The 12-month founding rate is a benefit, not a commitment — you can pause or close your listing at any time.
Is Mirr only for Australian brands?
For now, yes — we’re launching with Australian DTC brands and Australian shoppers. Once we’ve proven the model locally, we’ll expand. Founding brands will be front-of-queue as we open new markets.

Everything you need to know before listing your brand on Mirr.

When a customer purchases through Mirr, Stripe processes the full payment. 95% transfers to your brand account within 2 business days. We receive 5%. Automatic on every transaction — no invoicing, no chasing payments.
Flat-lay images work best — the garment laid flat on a plain surface, or hung on a hanger against a clean background. No models wearing the item. You'll also submit garment dimensions for the fitting room feature.
No. Mirr is an additional sales channel — like listing on a marketplace. Your own store stays exactly as it is. Mirr brings you customers who specifically want to try before they buy.
You ship directly to the customer. When a sale is made, you receive the order details and shipping address. You fulfil exactly as you would from your own store. Mirr handles payment and commission — you handle fulfilment.
Returns are handled between you and the customer per your policy. If a return results in a refund, the commission on that transaction is reversed automatically via Stripe. The goal of Mirr is to significantly reduce returns — customers who use try-on before buying are far more confident in their purchase.
Within 24 hours of submitting your product photos and dimensions. Our team reviews each submission to ensure garment images meet our quality standards. Once approved, your products are visible to all Mirr shoppers immediately.

HOW THE FIT SCORE WORKS

Your measurements.
Against the garment.

Every brand on Mirr submits the exact dimensions of each garment. When you enter your measurements, we compare them side by side and show you a fit score per dimension — so you know before you buy whether the shoulders will sit right, whether the length works for your height, and whether the chest will feel comfortable.
